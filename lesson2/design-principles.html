White Space and Clean Design

Archives of Nethys

2e.aonprd.com

The lines are neatly spaced, with room between them. Each section is clearly labeled with bold text highlighted in colors noting categories and subcategories. The font is clear and easy to read.

Alignment

Church of Jesus Christ

churchofjesuschrist.org

The thumbnails are all nicely aligned with each other both vertically and horizontally. This makes it look neat and organized, and helps draw the eyes.

Contrast

Laramie County School District

laramie1.org

The colors contrast sharply with each other, making the text stand out. It draws the eyes to the navigation categories and also helps to make the text easier to read.
